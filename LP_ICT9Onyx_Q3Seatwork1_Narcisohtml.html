THE BIG BANG THEORY

The Big Bang theory is about "weird and nerd" friends. They started their friendship in their apartments. It was a comedy tv show starring Jim Parsons (Main Character, Sheldon) Johnny Galeki as Leonard, Kely Cuoco as Penny and etc...

-THE CAST-
1   Sheldon Played by Jim Parsons Sheldon Cooper is the Main character of the TV show. He is known for his brillant mind, quirky behavior and a one of a kind attitude. He is also known for being one of the most sarcastic in the show. He has a wife (Amy). He is known for his catchphrase "Bazinga".
2   Penny Played by Kaley Cuoco Penny is the primary female lead. She lives in one of the apartments with her husband (Leonard). She is the first friend of Leonard and Sheldon, because she was the first person to enter the apartment besides both of them. She was very outgoing and was known for dating a lot of people throughout the show.
3   Leonard Played by Johnny Galecki Leonard is also one of the smartest in the show. He is called a extreme nerd and is also very outgoing. Although, he is awkward when it comes to his social life. He is almost as intelligent as sheldon.
4   Raj Played by Kunal Nayyar Raj is a person who is patient & level-headed. His friends bully him because of sense of fashion and enjoyment in cooking. He is an extremely shy person and an extemely silent person.
5   Amy Played by Mayim Bialik She is one of the strongest females in the show. She has a unique personality & is the wife of Sheldon Cooper.
6   Howard Played by Simon Helberg He is the most confident person in the show. He is a very good friend & has extreme fear of women rejecting him.
